About

I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at UNIST, advised by Prof. Kyungdon Joo. Currently, I am a Visiting Researcher at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), working with Prof. Deva Ramanan.

My research interests lie at the intersection of 3D-LLM, Vision-Language Models (VLM), and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. I am passionate about building systems that can understand and interact with open-vocabulary 3D scenes.
